Get started with MiMEngine

The MiMEngine is an open-source digital publishing platform for creators, writers, artists, animators, and techies that utilizes Generative AI, Conversational AI, and a blend of anime and manga to create a unique reader experience. M-motion I-interactive M-messaging E-experience (MiME)s, consist of Books and Characters wrapped up in a digital experience that allows the Reader to interact with elements within the MiMEs.

As a Creator, the MiMEngine offers a digital publishing platform. The platform consists of iOS and Android Apps that facilitate in-app purchases and subscriptions. The purchases range from single buys to monthly subscriptions and branded physical products. The Store within each App allows Readers to purchase MiMEs, subscribe to a collection of MiMEs, purchase branded physical products, and access Ad based free MiMEs.
